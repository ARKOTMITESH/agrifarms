Versatile Uses

Vetiver Applications

Vetiver is nature's most versatile plant — serving industries from perfumery to environmental conservation.

Aromatherapy

Grounding vetiver oil for stress relief, meditation, and holistic wellness practices.

Agriculture

Companion planting for pest control and sustainable organic farming systems.

Soil Erosion Control

Deep root system stabilizes soil on slopes, roadsides, and riverbanks effectively.

Water Conservation

Natural filtration and groundwater recharge through vetiver grass hedgerows.

Herbal Wellness

Traditional Ayurvedic medicine for cooling, calming, and anti-inflammatory properties.

Natural Cooling

Vetiver mats, curtains, and water coolers for chemical-free natural air cooling.

Perfume Industry

Base note in luxury fragrances, colognes, and high-end perfume formulations.

Eco-Friendly Projects

Green construction, landfill management, and ecological restoration programs.
