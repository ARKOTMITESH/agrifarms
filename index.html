Vetiver Benefits

Soil Erosion Control

Massive root systems anchor soil, preventing erosion on slopes, riverbanks, and agricultural land.

Water Conservation

Slowing down runoff and improving groundwater recharge through natural vegetative barriers.

Land Restoration

Reclaiming degraded soils, absorbing heavy metals, and restoring agricultural productivity.

Carbon Sequestration

High biomass potential capturing carbon dioxide, contributing to climate mitigation.

Essential Oil Production

Cultivating premium roots for extraction of high-value steam-distilled vetiver essential oil.

Environmental Protection

Natural bio-filtration grids helping mitigate pollution in wetlands and agricultural runoff.

Sustainable Income

Long-term cash flow and high profitability opportunities for partner farmers.
