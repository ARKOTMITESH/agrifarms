About Our Vetiver Agro Initiative

Mahima Agro Farms is India's trusted Vetiver cultivation, procurement, and supply network. We collaborate with farmers by providing premium Vetiver planting materials, technical guidance, field monitoring, and cultivation support. Our goal is to create a sustainable ecosystem where farmers receive professional assistance while buyers gain access to quality-assured Vetiver production.

Founded and directed by entrepreneur Mr. Sunil Kumar Pynam, Mahima Agro Farms was officially incorporated on January 10, 2025, launching our high-tech vetiver contract agricultural operations.

Today, under the leadership of Mr. Sunil Kumar Pynam, our farm network spans over 500+ acres of supported land, onboarding local farming communities and ensuring strict chemical-free organic practices. We bridge the gap between growers and global fragrance, cosmetics, and environmental protection projects — building long-term sustainable agriculture.

From Farm to You

Our Manufacturing Process

From the seed in the soil to the product in your hands — every step of our process is guided by quality, care, and respect for nature.

01
Sustainable Cultivation

We plant certified vetiver slips in carefully prepared, chemical-free soil. Using rainwater harvesting and natural composting, we nurture the plants to full maturity — a process that takes 12–18 months for premium quality.

02
Careful Harvesting

Our trained farmers hand-harvest vetiver roots at peak maturity, typically during the cool months for maximum aromatic potency. Roots are carefully uprooted to preserve their natural structure and essential oil content.

03
Natural Processing

Harvested roots are cleaned with pure water and shade-dried naturally — no artificial heat or chemical treatments. This traditional method preserves the full aromatic profile and therapeutic compounds unique to vetiver.

04
Quality Testing

Every batch undergoes rigorous quality testing — moisture content analysis, aromatic compound verification, microbial testing, and essential oil yield measurement — ensuring only the finest products proceed to packaging.

05
Export Packaging

Products are packed in eco-friendly, moisture-resistant export-grade packaging. Each consignment includes complete documentation — phytosanitary certificates, COA, MSDS, and organic certification copies.

06
Global Delivery

We handle complete logistics for both domestic and international shipments — sea freight, air cargo, and courier — with real-time tracking and dedicated account managers ensuring your order arrives safely and on time.
